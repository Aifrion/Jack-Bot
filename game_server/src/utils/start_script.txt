Hi everyone! Welcome to Jack Bot! A game where questions are asked and you and your friends answer them 
on your devices. But here is the thing, all but one of you will recieve the same question while that 
special one must try their best to give an answer that blends in. This person is the spy and it is 
up to the rest of the group to find out who that spy is. 

The questions that will be asked will have answers that fall between the numbers 0 to 10. After seeing
the question you be asked to fill in your answer on your device. Once everyone, including the spy, has
answered, I will read the question out loud and repeat your answer for everyone to hear. Everyone will 
have a chance to defend their answer and once that is over, everyone must vote on who the spy is. The
person with the most votes is eliminated. If the spy is elimited the players win. If the spy is remains
in the game along with one other player, the spy wins. 

Now good luck and you and especially good luck to the spy.
